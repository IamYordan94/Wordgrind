Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. This is a client-side word game that operates entirely in your browser.

The game saves your progress, discovered words, and current level in your browser's local storage. This data remains on your device and is not shared.

Third-Party Services

This game may use Google AdSense for advertisements. AdSense may collect anonymous usage data according to Google's privacy policies.

Dictionary API

Word definitions are fetched from the Free Dictionary API. No personal information is sent with these requests.

Data Security

All game data is stored locally on your device. We implement appropriate technical measures to protect the integrity of the game experience.
