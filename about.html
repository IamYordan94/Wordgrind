Progressive Word Grind is an educational word discovery game that challenges you to find every possible word of each length, starting from simple 2-letter combinations and progressively advancing to longer, more complex words.

Unlike traditional word games where you guess a single daily word, this game requires you to discover all valid words of a given length before unlocking the next level, creating an engaging "grind" experience with clear advancement milestones.

Level Progression System

The game features a unique progression system where completing all words of one length unlocks the next level:

  • Level 1 (2-letter words): Start with simple combinations like "at", "is", "up"
  • Level 2 (3-letter words): Progress to words like "cat", "run", "joy" (unlocked after completing 2-letter level)
  • Level 3+ (4-7+ letter words): Continue building vocabulary with increasingly complex words

Game Mechanics

Enter word guesses and receive immediate feedback:

  • Green letters: Correct letter in correct position
  • Yellow letters: Correct letter in wrong position
  • Gray letters: Letter not in the word
