How It Works

Progressive Word Grind challenges you to discover all possible words at each level before advancing to the next. Unlike traditional Wordle, this isn't about guessing one daily word - it's about systematic vocabulary building.

Game Flow:

  • Start with 2-letter words (160 total)
  • Find every possible word to unlock the next level
  • Progress through increasingly challenging levels up to 9-letter words
  • Use hints when you're stuck
  • Build comprehensive vocabulary knowledge
Word Levels & Lengths

The game spans 8 comprehensive levels covering 2-letter through 9-letter words:

Complete Level Breakdown:

  • Level 1: 2-letter words (160 words)
  • Level 2: 3-letter words (1,421 words)
  • Level 3: 4-letter words (5,273 words)
  • Level 4: 5-letter words (10,230 words)
  • Level 5: 6-letter words (17,708 words)
  • Level 6: 7-letter words (23,870 words)
  • Level 7: 8-letter words (28,419 words)
  • Level 8: 9-letter words (28,419 words)

Total: Over 115,000 words! This represents the complete ENABLE1 dictionary spanning 2-9 letter English words, including challenging 8 and 9-letter word levels.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions:

Q: Do I need to find ALL words to advance?
A: Yes! This creates a comprehensive learning experience and ensures you don't miss valuable vocabulary.

Q: What word dictionary is used?
A: We use the ENABLE1 dictionary, the same standard used by competitive word games like Scrabble.

Q: How do hints work?
A: Choose from definition hints, hangman-style letter reveals, or mix & match games. All hints have a 30-second cooldown for fair gameplay.

Q: Is my progress saved?
A: Yes, all progress is automatically saved in your browser's local storage.

Q: Are 8 and 9-letter words really included?
A: Absolutely! The game includes challenging levels up to 9-letter words with over 28,000 words each.
