About WordGrind

WordGrind is a progressive vocabulary learning game that guides you through discovering words from 2 to 9 letters. Build your mastery through a tier-based journey designed to reward dedication and growth.

How the Tier System Works

Start with the Free tier and progress through three levels:

  • Free Tier: 20 words per day, 500 lifetime cap, learn the fundamentals
  • Premium: Upgrade for $1.50/month to unlock 50 words/day with no cooldowns
  • Lifetime: Reach 58,535 words discovered and earn premium forever‚Äîfree!

Game Features

  • 117,000+ words across 8 progressive levels
  • Multiple hint systems: definitions, hangman, mix & match
  • Track your vocabulary journey with detailed progress stats
  • Offline-capable Progressive Web App (PWA)
  • No ads, clean interface, focused learning

Why Tier-Based Learning?

The tier system creates sustainable learning habits. Free players build consistency. Premium players accelerate progress. Lifetime achievers prove mastery through dedication‚Äîearning permanent access as a reward for discovering half the English language.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

Information We Collect

WordGrind stores your game progress locally in your browser's storage. We do not collect or transmit personal information to external servers.

Local Storage

The game saves your progress, discovered words, tier status, and current level in your browser's local storage. This data remains on your device and is not shared with third parties.

Payment Processing

Premium subscriptions are processed securely through Stripe. We do not store your payment information. Stripe's privacy policy governs payment data.

Dictionary API

Word definitions are fetched from the Free Dictionary API. No personal information is sent with these requests‚Äîonly the word being looked up.

Data Security

All game data is stored locally on your device. We implement appropriate technical measures to protect the integrity of the game experience. Your tier status and word count are maintained in browser storage.

No Advertising

WordGrind does not use advertising networks or tracking pixels. We do not sell user data or display ads.
