About Word Grind

Word Grind is an educational word discovery game that challenges you to find all possible words at each level before advancing to the next.

How It Works

  • Start with 2-letter words and progress to 9-letter words
  • Discover every word to unlock the next level
  • Use hints: definitions, hangman, and mix & match games
  • Build comprehensive vocabulary knowledge

Educational Benefits

  • Systematic vocabulary building
  • Pattern recognition and word formation
  • Memory reinforcement through repetition
  • Perfect for students and word game enthusiasts

Game Features

  • Over 115,000 words across 8 levels
  • Live dictionary definitions
  • Offline gameplay capability
  • Progress tracking and persistence

Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

Information We Collect

Word Grind stores your game progress locally in your browser's storage. We do not collect or transmit personal information.

Local Storage

The game saves your progress, discovered words, and current level in your browser's local storage. This data remains on your device and is not shared.

Third-Party Services

This game may use Google AdSense for advertisements. AdSense may collect anonymous usage data according to Google's privacy policies.

Dictionary API

Word definitions are fetched from the Free Dictionary API. No personal information is sent with these requests.

Data Security

All game data is stored locally on your device. We implement appropriate technical measures to protect the integrity of the game experience.
